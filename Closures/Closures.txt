Def. 1) A Closure is a function bundled together with its parent's lexical scope.
Def. 2) A function along with its lexical scope bundled together forms a Closure.

Uses of Closures:-
1.) Module Design Pattern
2.) Currying
3.) functions like once
4.) memoize
5.) maintaining state in async world
6.) setTimeouts
7.) Iterators
etc.
